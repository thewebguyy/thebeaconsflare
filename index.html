Gen Z Worship

Gen Z Worship is a platform that seeks to educate the Generation Z on the culture of Worship as a lifestyle and this will be accomplished in an atmosphere where anointed Psalmist will converge and release sounds of intimacy which will be coupled with the Truth of God's Word taught accurately, thereby bringing understanding to a generation.

Teens Pray 2024

Teens Pray aims at raising a Praying Generation by the teaching of the Word of God premised on a dense atmosphere of the Spirit generated through prayers.

Lightroom

The Lightroom is a quarterly meeting that grooms, equips, and deploys young men and women into their prophetic assignment. It is God's Lecture arena that prepares men for every good work and launches them out for the ultimate establishment of the Kingdom of God. At the Lightroom, Samuel is able to discern the voice of God and respond dutifully.
